Miso Soup

Ingredients:

Amount Ingredient
4 Cups Vegetable broth
2 tsp Kelp granules
8 Oz Tofu
2 Green onions

Instructions:

1. Combine broth and kelp granules in a saucepan on medium heat until it reaches a boil. While the broth is heating, slice the tofu and green onions into a desired size.
2. Turn the heat down to simmer and mix in the miso paste.
3. Add the tofu and green onions and heat for five minutes.
