Chili

Ingredients:

Amount Ingredient
4 Cans Beans
1⁄2 Orange
3 Cloves Garlic
1 Onion
1-2 Soyrizo
1 large can Diced tomatoes
1 bottle or can Beer
1 can Corn (optional)
To taste Soy sauce
To taste Cumin
To taste Chili powder
To taste Hot sauce
To taste Salt & pepper

Instructions:

1. Sauté onions in olive oil in large pot until clear.
2. Add garlic and continue cooking for 3 minutes.
3. Add soyrizo and cook for 3 more minutes.
4. Add tomatoes, drained beans, beer, orange juice, corn, and stock of water so liquid is about 1 inch above ingredients.
5. Boil on low until right consistency, about 40 minutes.
6. Add spices to taste and serve.
